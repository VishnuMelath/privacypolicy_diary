My Personal Diary App

Your private space for offline journaling and event planning

Description

My Personal Diary is an Android app that prioritizes your privacy. It operates offline, allowing you to record your daily events, milestones, and upcoming plans without relying on external servers.

Key Features

  • Offline Functionality
  • Secure Local Storage
  • Personalized Entries
  • Upcoming Events Planner
  • Customization Options
  • Passcode Protection

Permissions

  • Storage: Required for saving diary entries and images locally on your device.
  • Calendar: Used for creating and managing upcoming events within the app.

Privacy Assurance

My Personal Diary prioritizes user privacy and does not collect, store, or share any personal information externally. Your data remains confidential and accessible only to you.
